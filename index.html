About Me

I am a computer scientist specializing in algorithm engineering, parallel computing, and advanced computer systems. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science (2008) from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, I am passionate about developing scalable, high-performance solutions for complex computing challenges. I live in central Ohio, USA.
