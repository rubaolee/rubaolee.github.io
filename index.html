About Me

I am a computer scientist with a deep interest in algorithm engineering, computer systems, and parallel computing. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. With over 20 years of experience, I have made extensive contributions to high-performance computing, GPU utilization, and database systems.
